## Scope of estimates

All figures produced by this tool are **gross payroll estimates**. They do not account for:

- employee morale, engagement, or trust
- turnover, attrition, or recruitment and onboarding cost
- care-delivery, quality, or patient-safety impact
- the administrative or timekeeping-system cost of implementing a program
- tax, benefits, or accounting treatment beyond the optional employer payroll-tax (FICA) uplift

The optional payroll-tax uplift reflects the employer FICA match only and is a simplification.

## Voluntariness and equity

Every scenario in this tool assumes participation is genuinely voluntary. A program that is voluntary in name only is a materially different thing than what this tool models.

Voluntary salary reductions for salaried exempt employees may require written agreements and can carry state-level notice requirements under the Fair Labor Standards Act and applicable state law. The tool flags where this applies but is not a substitute for legal and HR review.
